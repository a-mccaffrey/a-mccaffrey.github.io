About Me

I am an EU-Canadian citizen and front-end developer who is permanently relocating to Luxembourg and I'm currently seeking employment on the client-side or at an agency where I can apply my experience with graphic design, video editing, web design, and coding. I have an honours-level Bachelor's Degree in International Business from Carleton University, and will soon complete a full-stack web development certificate from the same institution.

Professionally, I have 4+ years of B2B marketing experience in multiple countries. I have worked on web development projects with remote and on-site teams contributing to the UX and front-end development that resulted in tangible business outcomes, and am known for creative problem solving by my team members. In my career, I have been responsible for graphic design as a major or minor part of my role for 4+ years with Illustrator and Photoshop, and I have worked on video editing and animation for both my full-time role and freelance using Premiere Pro and Open Shot. I have worked with leading marketing and sales platforms including Hubspot and Salesforce and I would like to apply my perspective to delivering value for my future clients. I played a leadership role in the rebranding of a top 100 tech company in Canada and over my career, I have successfully managed projects for campaigns, branding, website development, integration of marketing tools, marketing analytics, and social media advertising.
